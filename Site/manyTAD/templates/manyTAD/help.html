Interpreting Results From TADMaster

Introduction

TADMaster provides an analysis suite to directly evaluate the concordance level and robustness of two or more TADs data for a genome region. This article will discuss how a user can the analysis to interpret the similarities and differences of the TADs data. The focus of this tutorial will be analyzing the results of multiple TADS identified from chromosome 19 (GEO #GSE35156). It is encourage that one follows along with the tutorial by using the following link: chromosome 19 (GEO #GSE35156)
